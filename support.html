FAQ

Is VYRA+ a lottery?
No. VYRA+ is a rewards club. Membership value is provided independent of draws.
Is free entry available?
Yes. Free entry is always available and treated equally (subject to T&Cs).
How do I cancel?
Manage your membership inside Dashboard (production) or contact support.
How do you verify winners?
Winner announcements are livestreamed and posted with consent in Winner Proof.
